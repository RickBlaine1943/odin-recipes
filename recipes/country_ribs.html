h1>Slow and Low Country Ribs

Description

BBQ Pork "Country Ribs" - cut from the shoulder, cooked slow and low, and glazed with the barbecue sauce of your choice! This country-style ribs recipe teaches you all you need to know.

Ingredients

    4 country ribs, about 3 pounds
    Kosher salt
    Vegetable oil
    The barbecue sauce of your choice

Steps

    Cut and salt the ribs:
    Slow cook the ribs for 90 min to start
    Every 30 minutes or so, turn your ribs and paint them again with the sauce:
    Move to hot side of grill or broil:
